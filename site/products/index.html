CubeBuild

CubeBuild is a CRM for small organisations.

CubeBuild provides:

  • Web based online point of sale.
  • Stock management and tracking.
  • Sales reporting with GST (goods and services tax).
  • Shipping, both pricing and logistics management.
  • Stock replenishment.

CubeBuild is built using the Microsoft.NET Framework v4.7.
